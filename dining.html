Summary of our Facilities

5-STAR, CLASSIC 5-COURSE GOURMET DINNER IN OUR EUROPEAN STYLE DINING ROOM OR AS ROOM SERVICE BROUGHT TO YOU AND CLASSIC STYLE BREAKFAST IN-BED.

Gastronomic 5 Course Meal: 1 amuse bouche, 2 Starters (either Appy, Soup or Salad), 1 main Entree Choice and 1 Dessert Choice.

Vegan and Vegetarian dishes are available, we offer a large array of choices from Heart Smart,Gluten Free and Celiac Disease on your request.

DINING ROOM OPEN - 6:00 pm to 8:00 pm (to 9:00 pm Fridays). Reserve Table and Place Order Between 12pm - 5pm. 866-202-1179 ext 108
